Chamber Site Plan

Site: Name:

HsinChu Root Chamber of Commerce

I selected this site name is because I was born in HsinChu, raised in HsinChu and develop my carrier in HsinChu. It is like a root of everything in my life. That's the reason I choosed HsinChu Root.

Site Purpose:

The mission of the Hsischu Root Chamber of Commerce website is to promote and support the prosperity and development of our business community. We are committed to providing a platform for sharing resources and information, allowing our members and business partners to connect, collaborate, and grow together. Our goal is to facilitate mutual benefit in business, offer training and educational opportunities, and establish strong connections within the local community to achieve shared success.

Scenarios:

  • What benefits will you get from joining this chamber of commerce?
  • What are the products in high demand in this region?
  • What activities have been held recently for merchants to communicate with each other?
